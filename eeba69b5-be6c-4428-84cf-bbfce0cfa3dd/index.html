Guidelines for Antarctica

Planing to sail to Anractica? IAATO has just release a short animated briefing to supplement the existing Mandatory BriefingVisitors Guide to the Antarctic which is presented by IAATO operators to guests and crew before arriving in Antarctica to make them aware of necessary safety and environmental requirements. 

Briefing Film - Going Ashore Visitors (English) from IAATO on Vimeo.

One can wonder why they have used a rather boring animation to illustrate the guidelines, but the answer may be exactly that: Boring.

If they had used footage from Antarctica the viewer might just get distracted from the message in the video.
